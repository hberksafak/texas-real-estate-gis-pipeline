## Why Top 25 Changed After V2

The Top 25 changed after the scoring upgrade because v1 was a coarse proxy
model. The frozen v2 model added waterbody exclusion, stronger constraint
avoidance, and more explicit neutral spatial/context scoring. School district
context was removed from scoring and kept as neutral metadata only. Opportunity
Zone influence was reduced to a small policy/incentive context factor. Geometry
validity became a QA requirement rather than a positive scoring bonus.

The final v2 model is frozen and reproducible for this portfolio release.

## Analytical Limitations

- Candidate polygons are analyst-defined grid proxies, not official parcels.
- This is not an acquisition recommendation.
- No ownership, parcel valuation, zoning, utilities, legal access, engineering,
  FEMA flood, road accessibility, or NCTCOG land-use suitability is included.
- Basemap tiles are visualization only.
- School district context is neutral context only.
- Opportunity Zones are policy/incentive context only.
- No demographic targeting or protected-class logic is used.
